About

I am currently a Thonis Family Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Earth and Environmental Sciences at Syracuse University.

Prior to this, I obtained my doctorate in Geology at Peking University.

My current research focuses on redox proxy validation and environmental reconstruction through Earth System Modeling (cGENIE).
